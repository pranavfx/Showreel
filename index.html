Video Editor | VFX Artist | FilmMaker








Dynamic Matchmove Artist with over 9+ years of experience in the motion picture and film industry, specializing in advanced visual effects and animation. Demonstrated ability to deliver high-quality results on complex VFX and animation projects using industry-standard tools such as 3DEqualizer, Maya, and Nuke. Experienced in video editing and post-production using Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve, contributing to editorial, color correction, and final delivery as well as on-set shoot experience for short films and commercial content. Known for a detail-oriented, collaborative approach and a strong balance of technical accuracy and creative problem-solving.
